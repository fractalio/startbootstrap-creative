Architecture


The IntegralStor architecture, based on the latest generation energy-efficient hardware and fractalio’s IntegralView storage management software, delivers a scalable, high-performance, reliable and easy-to-manage storage solution.

Flexible deployment & incremental growth

Eliminate the industry-prevalent, expensive and wasteful practice of over-provisioned storage infrastructure. IntegralView, our web enabled single-dashboard storage manager, aggregates and virtualizes multiple storage pools into a unified namespace, allowing a single storage system or a grid of storage-systems to be configured, administered & monitored as a single system, allowing capacity or performance to be seamlessly added, only when required.

In the decade and more that we've been involved in the data storage domain, we've had a ringside view of several generations of data storage solutions, attempting to address the challenge of persistent & durable electronic data storage.

We also have the acute insight, that apart from peformance and capacity, nothing very significant has changed in terms of data durability.

Data Durability and Content Integrity

The challenge of decoupling data durablity and content integrity from the underlying hardware remains. While the cloud has abstracted away some of the hardware dependency, it has come at the cost of security and privacy, but still does not deliver true hardware­decoupled data durability.

Reclaim simplicity,

we have developed a new design that mitigates the complexity, costs and limited lifespans of traditional data storage infrastructure.

Algorithmic Data Representation

Our patent­pending algorithmic data representation and storage system is a fundamentally new, future­proof approach to information storage, delivering an internet- scale framework that guarantees data content integrity, security & privacy, as well as hitherto unprecedented levels of efficiency in data footprint optimization, transmission and archival.

  • (Hi)story

    • Our engagement with all things data storage goes back over a decade to the year 2000, when Datalifecycle was start as an independent system builder of data storage solutions, using hardware and software building blocks from a number of innovative vendors from across the world.

      Over these many years, while we have been able to adopt commodity hardware for data storage fairly early, we've been dependent, for a much longer time, on third-party vendors, some proprietary and some open, for storage software.
    • In 2014, we decided to take the logical step of further backward integration, and develop our own storage storage platform using a base of different open source projects.

    Fractalio Data was conceived in response to the challenge of containing and reliably managing the explosion of electronic data that IT enabled organizations of all sizes are experiencing.

    Fractalio Data is led by a team of industry veterans spanning technology, engineering and business, who started off over a decade ago as vendor-independent system integrators of data storage solutions, and were among the few Indian companies to design and deliver solutions addressing the full spectrum of “Datalifecycle” requirements, from data storage, through archival, retrieval and elimination; from in-premise to the cloud.

    In addition to, and of equal importance as our data storage initiative, as individuals and as a group, we're interested in a wider spectrum of technology - its role, influence and impact on sustenance, convenience and leisure, and consequently then, on livelihoods and the nature and role of work in society. In more concrete terms, we're interested in harnessing technology to address local needs towards sustainable living in the areas of food, water, energy security & shelter, and strive to provide an environment that supports explorations in these areas.

    So, our data storage business is but one dimension, in a much broader set of motivations, and serves as an enabler to facilitate these explorations.

    Our engineering team is based in a very picturesque rural area about 40km west of Bangalore city, and we currently maintain business operations in Bangalore & New Delhi.
